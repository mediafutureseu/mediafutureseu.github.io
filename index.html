Data Infrastructure for Experiment Support

This website is a repository of relevant resources for the projects to be selected in the 1st Open Call of Mediafutures, an EU-funded virtual European data innovation hub to support entrepreneurial and innovative projects to reshape the media value chain. The catalogue of datasets provides different data assets of interest for the challenges on coronavirus and misinformation.

The repository also includes lists of technological tools suggested by Mediafutures mentors for startups and artists to collect new data, analyze and visualize datasets in order to create their projects on data, technology and arts.
